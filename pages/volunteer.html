Keep the food and earn Hasanat!

1- About the idea:

Lots of good food are going to rubbish after the end of Iftar meeting in Ramadan especially in hotels and restaurants.


And from here our idea born, we offer a volunteering activity for those youths who want to gain Hasanat in Ramadan to join us and participate in daily events in different hotels and restaurant.


Our volunteers will help in arrange excess foods from Iftar meals and organize them in another meals to give it to poor people.
